Serene Shrooms

Description

A chorus of interactive giant mushrooms, each playing a unique low meditative drone note, together form a soothing sound bath.

Mushrooms are lit! This installation is raising awareness about the unique beauty and variety of the fruiting bodies (mushrooms) of a network of subterranean mycellium that communicate with each other.
Somewhere between plants and animals, mycellium break down decaying materials into nutrients and transmit them below the forest floor in a wood wide web where they are needed for new growth.

Technical Details

  • Dimensions: Colony of eight mushrooms in 2.5 x 10 x 15 meter space
  • Materials: Polyurethane Foam
  • Interactivity: Touch
  • Technology: ESP-32-S3, MusicMaker, Lidar sensor array, Addressable LED pebble and strip lighting. custom C++ code
  • Fabrication Time: 18 months
  • Budget: Materials: $4500, Large Labor of Love
