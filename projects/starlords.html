StarLords

Description

StarLords is an original, large scale, four player, retro style video game designed for large social gatherings and makes use of the BigPixel display. Defend your Space Fortress from the destructive Plasma Ball!

StarLords has been featured at multiple high-profile Cleveland events, engaging thousands of visitors in collaborative play. The game's retro aesthetic and social gameplay mechanics create memorable experiences that bridge generations and encourage teamwork among strangers.
