Description

BigPixel is a large and beautiful lighting display made of up-cycled one gallon empty plastic water jugs. The lightweight and modular design allows for easy transport and assembly into different shapes. Brought to you by Makers Alliance, Ingenuity Ingeneers, think[box] and Plastipak.
