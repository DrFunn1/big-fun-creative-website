BigPixel Display

Description

BigPixel is a large and beautiful lighting display made of up-cycled one gallon empty plastic water jugs. The lightweight and modular design allows for easy transport and assembly into different shapes.

This innovative installation transforms recycled materials into a stunning interactive display system, demonstrating the creative potential of sustainable design while providing a versatile platform for digital art and gaming experiences.

Technical Details

  • Dimensions: 2x2-1/2x1 foot panels make a modular system, configurable to various sizes
  • Materials: Up-cycled one-gallon plastic water jugs and coraplast frames, LED lighting, custom electronics
  • Interactivity: Programmable LED matrix for games, animations, and visual displays
  • Technology: LED matrix control via ESP-32 running as ArtNet node, real-time graphics rendering, modular assembly system
  • Fabrication Time: 12 months
  • Sustainability: Built from recycled materials, demonstrating eco-friendly design principles
