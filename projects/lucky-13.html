Lucky 13

Description

Lucky 13 blends old school casino and modern electronics to provide a jackpot of positivity, with affirmations and a chance to shake your booty! The installation encourages play and social interaction, inviting participants to take a chance and maybe do a dance!

The square graphics that make up the spinning reels can be customized for different events. Jackpot rewards can also be updated to fit event themes.

Technical Details

  • Dimensions: 7x6x9 feet
  • Materials: Wood, electronics
  • Interactivity: Slot machine pull handle, approach sensor
  • Technology: PC running node javascript, johny five driving arduino nano relays for old school light bulb animations
  • Fabrication Time: 3 months
  • Budget: $1,000
